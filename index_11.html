Structural Engineering

Our Structural Engineers thrive when facing complex engineering problems. Having a can-do attitude, using advanced design and analysis tools combined with traditional engineering judgement, we undertake all types and scales of projects — from simple floor layout alterations to new builds, warehouses or multi-storey basements.

Architectural Design

We are specialised in residential architecture and help our clients unlock the potential of their homes through extensions, conversions or renovations. Our multi-disciplinary teams work together to bring holistic solutions to complex situations — selected for creativity, aesthetic acumen and technical skills.

Planning & Building Control

We have extensive experience working with both planning and building control authorities, with a positive track record of successfully approved projects. Using our engineering capacity and our architects' thorough Building Regulations knowledge, we ensure your project gets approved without delays.
